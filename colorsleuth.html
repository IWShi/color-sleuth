Color Sleuth

Rules

Color Sleuth is a two-player game. Each turn, players will attempt to choose the colored tile that is a different shade from all the others.
If you choose correctly, you earn one point. If you choose incorrectly, you score no points.
The player with the most points after ten rounds wins.
As the rounds go on, the difficulty level will increase, so be prepared.

Once you are ready, press START to begin.
